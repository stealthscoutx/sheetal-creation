Odissi

Odissi is a dance to be enjoyed by all those who wish to have an experience that goes beyond entertainment, whether as a spectator or as a practitioner.

Odissi is literally an adventure that puts us in the role of explorer along the paths of our own body, mind and spirit. It is a style of classical and expressive dance originating from the state of Orissa, India. An art that combines dance, theater, music and literature in a sensitive and poetic way.

Sheetal Sivangi

She is an accomplished Odissi dancer with over 18 years of experience. A Gold Medalist in Classical Dance, she has performed at numerous national festivals and has been honoured with prestigious titles such as Nritya Shiromani and Nrityashree National Award.

Along with her rich performance journey, she has been a dedicated dance educator, nurturing students across India and abroad.
